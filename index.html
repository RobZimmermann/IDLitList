The aim of this document is to provide a comprehensive list of literature that can help with morphological identification of European bees and aculeate wasps. Since different perspectives can be helpful at the beginning of working with the fauna, not only larger works with keys, but also smaller publications are included as they can contain very useful information. If no link to a PDF version is given here, try Zobodat or GoogleScholar.

Thanks to Manuel Schulz (iNat @naturschulz), Joan Díaz Calafat (iNat @jdiazcalafat), and Justyna Kierat (iNat @justyna_kierat) for additions.
